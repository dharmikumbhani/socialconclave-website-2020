CONCLAVE
2020

Dear Delegates,
You must be aware of the coronavirus epidemic. Thousands across the world have lost their lives due to the deadly virus. Unfortunately, the virus has now struck Mumbai with two cases of coronavirus being reported today. Hence, keeping the safety of the delegates and the eminent speakers in mind, we have had to take the difficult decision of cancelling Social Conclave 2020. This decision was taken as per the instructions we received from the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Government of Maharashtra and India in due consultation with the NMIMS Management.
It is with utmost despair that we have had to inform you about this decision so close to the conference. I hope you understand that this was something beyond our hands.
Please be rest assured, we will completely refund the delegate fees in the span of next 20 days. The team will be there to assist all your queries and you needn’t be worried about your delegate fees. We understand that you would have already made plans and you invested a considerable amount of time and energy on Social Conclave 2020. This has been an extremely difficult decision for the entire organizing committee and hence, we request your kind co-operation in this moment of grief.
Please feel free to contact us anytime in case of any queries.

Best regards and apologies,

Team Social Conclave
